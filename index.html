Research Summary

I'm widely interested in programming languages, formal methods, and theories about logic and computation. In particular, I focus on the following two directions.

  • Compilers and program analysis, with the goal of helping the software development process and improving software reliability / performance
  • Theory/model-independent aspects of logic and computation, with the goal of obtaining fundamental results with broad implications on computer science, mathematics, and philosophy
