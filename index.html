Background and Theme

My research has two directions: (1) program analysis and optimization, which automatically improve software reliability and performance; (2) mathematical logic and theory of computation, which study universal properties of human/computer's reasoning activities. Those two directions are related via the computational hardness of program analysis / transformation. Besides, I'm also widely interested in computer-aided theorem proving, algorithms and complexity, etc.
