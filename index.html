Theme

I focus on two directions.

  • Program analysis and optimization. I'm interested in widely-used language/compiler platforms (e.g. LLVM) and automatic reasoning tools (e.g. SMT solvers). Goal: practical impact on software reliability and performance.
  • Theory of computation. I study abstract and logical aspects of computation (e.g. intensional/extensional semantics of programs, computability, and some aspects of mathematical logic). Goal: theoretical impact on logic and philosophy, pure math, and computer science (in particular, artificial intellegence is also subject to theory of computation).
Those two directions are related via the computational hardness of program analysis (which is often undecidable, so complexity theory is insufficient).
