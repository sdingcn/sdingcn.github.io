Background and Theme

Logic has been an area of study since ancient time, and nowadays it still exhibits influence. (1) In computer science, formal methods based on logic improves reliability of hardware and software. (2) In mathematics and philosophy, logic reveals inherent properties about human's and computer/AI's reasoning activities via theorems like Gödel's incompleteness theorems and undecidability of the halting problem. My research is generally based on logic and concerns mainly the following areas.

  • Fully automated formal methods, focusing on program analysis and compilers/interpreters
  • Theory of computation, focusing on computability theory and relations between computations and proofs

I'm also broadly interested in related areas, such as computer-aided theorem proving, computational commplexity theory, etc.
